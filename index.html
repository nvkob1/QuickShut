Frequently Asked Questions

Is QuickShut free to use?

Yes, QuickShut is completely free to download and use with no hidden fees or premium features.

Which operating systems are supported?

QuickShut is currently designed for Windows operating systems. Support for macOS and Linux may be added in future updates.

Can I schedule recurring shutdowns?

The current version doesn't support recurring schedules, but this feature is planned for a future update.

Will QuickShut save my unsaved work before shutting down?

QuickShut uses Windows' standard shutdown procedure, which will prompt you to save unsaved work in most applications. However, we recommend saving your work regularly when a shutdown is scheduled.

Can I also schedule restarts or sleep mode?

Currently, QuickShut only supports shutdown operations. Restart and sleep mode options are planned for future releases.
